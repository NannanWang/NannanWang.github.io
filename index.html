Nannan Wang 王楠楠 ( CV ) 

  PhD Candidate

  School of Electronic Engineering

  Xidian University, Xi'an, China
I am a PhD candidate of Xidian University, Xi'an, China. I received my BSc. Degree (2009) in Infromation and Computation Science, Department of Applied Mathematics and Applied Physics, Xi'an University of Posts and Telecommunications. Then I came to Xidian University for my Master degree in Signal and Information Processing under the supervision of Prof. Xinbo Gao from Aug. 2009. From Sep. 2010, I began to pursue my doctor degree in VIPSL lab supervised by Prof. Xinbo Gao. From Sep. 2011 to Sep. 2013, I am a visiting PhD student at University of Technology, Sydney. My research interests are mainly on computer vision and machine learning.
